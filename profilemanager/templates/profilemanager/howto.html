How to play

We detect a disturbance in the force, and require your knowledge and wit to uncover it!


The game is composed of 3 initial stages and a final stage.

Initial stages

Stages 1-3 require you to play a multiple choice-based trivia game, each stage in increasing difficulty; Jedi Apprentice, Jedi Knight and Jedi master with 5 questions per level, and 30 seconds per question to answer, the faster you select the correct answer the greater the point bonus will be. To also aid you in the final stage; in each difficulty level if you succeed in answering all 5 questions correctly you will uncover various artefacts which serve as extra lives in the final stage.

Final stage

The final stage will involve you battling the mystery disturbance detected, in the force engaging you in a Rock, Paper, Scissors style strategy game. The user will have the option of using any of the following strategems in their battle against evil:

Force Shield (rock)

Blaster (scissors)

Lightsaber (paper)

Characters

You will have a choice of 5 characters to choose to play as. Pick wisely as each character have their own perks while completing the trivia:

  • Yoda - Force Sense; on activating this perk you will automatically detect the correct answer.
  • - Force Meditation; on activating this perk you will gain additional time to answer a question giving you the potential to get bonus point.
  • Darth Vader - Dark Force; on activating this perk you will gain bonus points for answering correctly.
  • Princess Leia - Force Empathy; on activating this perk you will be able to change the question asked.
  • Han Solo - Expert Markman; on activating this perk, two incorrect answers will be eliminated.

Hall of Fame

To reach the leaderboards you will need to be both swift and precise. Each question answered correctly will give you 10pt, the quicker you answer them will multiply the points and a difficulty factor of 1, 2 or 3.
